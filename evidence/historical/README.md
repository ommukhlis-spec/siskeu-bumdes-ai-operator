These artifacts prove that a **real BUMDes customer paid for the development of
the Siskeu BUMDes platform** prior to the XPRIZE window. They establish
**validated demand** and **paying-customer history**.

They are **NOT** submitted as XPRIZE-period AI Operator revenue. The original
development payment predates the competition and is not tied to the new AI
Operator service layer.

Fill in `manifest.json` (copy from `manifest.example.json`). **Never commit raw,
un-redacted financial or personal data.** `.gitignore` blocks the real files; only

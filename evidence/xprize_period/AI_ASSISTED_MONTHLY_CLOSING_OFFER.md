# Offer — AI-Assisted Monthly Closing & Report Briefing

## Offer Name

```text
AI-Assisted Monthly Closing & Report Briefing
```

## Target Customer

BUMDes operators, village-owned enterprise administrators, or small business finance administrators who need help understanding monthly financial reports and preparing internal report explanations.

## Problem

Monthly financial administration can be difficult to explain clearly without accounting or professional support. Operators may need help identifying missing data, understanding changes in revenue and expenses, preparing plain-language explanations, and documenting review steps.

## Service Description

Siskeu BUMDes AI Operator provides an AI-assisted monthly report briefing service. The system uses Gemini on Google Cloud to generate a plain-language explanation of a synthetic, redacted, or customer-approved monthly report. LynkMesh provides deterministic application context to ground the AI workflow, and each run is saved as an ExecutionLog for review.

## Included Deliverables

1. One AI-assisted monthly report briefing.
2. Plain-language summary of the monthly report.
3. Notable changes and watch items.
4. Human-reviewed result status.
5. Linked AI Operator run ID.
6. Public-safe summary or internal briefing document.

## Safety Boundary

```text
AI explains, checks, and proposes.
Human operators approve final financial decisions.
The system does not post ledger entries automatically.
The service is not a certified audit.
```

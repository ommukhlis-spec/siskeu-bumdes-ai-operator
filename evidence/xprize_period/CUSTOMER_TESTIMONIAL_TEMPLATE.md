## Feedback Questions

1. What financial administration problem did you want help with?
2. Did the AI-assisted briefing make the monthly report easier to understand?
3. Which part was most useful: summary, notable changes, watch items, or human review?
4. Would you consider using this as a monthly support service?
5. May we use a redacted version of your feedback for the XPRIZE submission?

## Redaction Notes

Remove:

- names,
- phone numbers,
- addresses,
- account numbers,
- private financial details,
- internal disputes or sensitive remarks,
- any personally identifiable data without permission.
